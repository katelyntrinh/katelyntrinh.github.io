Moon Milk Café

Website Concept

For my web design project 2, I used the Cosmic Comics story by Italo Calvino as my concept for my site. I wanted my website to resemble a restaurant website where you are able to see a menu of different food and drink items made with moon milk, one of the subjects discussed in the short story. I wanted to make my website very fun and tell the story of how they went to the moon and got the milk to make cheese.
