Graphics & Images

by Katelyn Trinh

Katelyn Trinh is an aspiring undergraduate student at California State University, East Bay working towards her BFA in graphic design. When it comes to her artistic capability and her passion, she enjoys playing with childlike concepts and bright colors that are fun and eye-catching. She is constantly inspired by the art and imagination of The Walt Disney Company and her current employer, The LEGO group which has continued to contribute to her artistic sense and her professional work ethic for the last 4 years. She is eager to continue working hard in her studies for web design, illustration, and graphic arts.
